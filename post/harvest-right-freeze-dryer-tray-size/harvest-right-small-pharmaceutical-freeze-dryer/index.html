3. The freeze dryer is not drying the product:

4. The freeze dryer is making noise:

  • Ensure that the condenser coils are clean.

5. The freeze dryer is leaking:

  • Check for any leaks in the vacuum lines.

  • Make sure that the drain pan is properly emptied.
