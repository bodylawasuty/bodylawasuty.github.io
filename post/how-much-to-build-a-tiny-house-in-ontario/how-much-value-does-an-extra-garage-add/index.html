A garage isn’t just a parking spot for your car anymore. It’s an extra room that can be used for storage, a workshop, or even a home gym. Not to mention, it can add value to your home.

How much value does an extra garage add?

It depends on a number of factors, including the location of your home, the size of the garage, and the condition of the garage. But on average, an extra garage can add anywhere from 3% to 10% to the value of your home.

Here are some real-life examples of how an extra garage can increase the value of a home:

  • In a recent study, homes with an extra garage sold for an average of $20,000 more than homes without an extra garage.
  • In another study, homes with an extra garage had an average appraised value that was $15,000 higher than homes without an extra garage.
  • And in a third study, homes with an extra garage sold for an average of 5% more than homes without an extra garage.

So, if you’re thinking about adding an extra garage to your home, it’s definitely worth considering. It could add a significant amount of value to your home.

Why do extra garages add value to homes?

There are a few reasons why extra garages add value to homes. First, they provide additional storage space. This is a major selling point for many homebuyers, especially those with families or hobbies that require a lot of gear.

Second, extra garages can be used as a workshop or a home gym. This is a great way to add extra living space to your home without having to add on to your house.

Third, extra garages can simply make your home more appealing to potential buyers. A home with an extra garage is more likely to be seen as a well-maintained and up-to-date property.

Is an extra garage worth the investment?

Whether or not an extra garage is worth the investment depends on a number of factors, such as your budget, your needs, and the value of your home. But if you’re looking for a way to add value to your home, an extra garage is definitely worth considering.

Key Takeaways:

  • Extra garages can add value to homes by providing additional storage space, creating a workshop or home gym, and making the home more appealing to potential buyers.
  • The amount of value an extra garage adds depends on factors such as the location of the home, the size of the garage, and the condition of the garage.
  • On average, an extra garage can add anywhere from 3% to 10% to the value of a home.
  • Extra garages are worth the investment for homeowners who are looking to add value to their home and increase its appeal to potential buyers.

Adding an extra garage to your property can be a smart investment, potentially increasing its value and functionality. But before you embark on this project, it’s crucial to analyze the costs and potential ROI.

Homebuyer Preferences#

Homebuyers often seek properties with extra garages for various reasons:

  • Storage space: An extra garage can provide ample storage for vehicles, tools, equipment, and other belongings.

  • Convenience: Having a dedicated space for parking and storage eliminates the need to park on the street or in a crowded driveway.

  • Increased curb appeal: An extra garage can enhance the overall look and feel of your property, potentially attracting more buyers.

Return on Investment Calculation#

To determine the ROI of adding an extra garage, consider the following steps:

  • Calculate the construction costs: Factor in expenses such as materials, labor, permits, and any additional features you desire.

  • Estimate the increase in property value: Research comparable properties in your area with extra garages to determine the potential appreciation.

  • Subtract the construction costs from the estimated increase in value: The result is the potential ROI.

Factors Affecting ROI#

Several factors can influence the ROI of an extra garage:

  • Location: The desirability of your neighborhood and the demand for properties with extra garages can significantly impact ROI.

  • Size and condition: A larger, well-maintained extra garage will likely yield a higher ROI than a smaller, rundown one.

  • Functionality: Consider whether the extra garage can serve multiple purposes, such as a workshop or home office. Added functionality can enhance ROI.

Key Takeaways:#

  • An extra garage can increase a property’s value by providing additional storage, convenience, and curb appeal.

  • Homebuyers often prefer properties with extra garages.

  • Calculating ROI involves subtracting construction costs from the estimated increase in property value.

  • Factors such as location, size, condition, and functionality can influence ROI.

FAQ#

Q1: To what extent does an additional garage increase the overall worth of a property?#

A1: The value added to a property by an extra garage can vary substantially depending on factors such as the local real estate market, the size and type of the garage, and the overall condition of the property. However, studies have shown that, on average, an extra garage can increase the value of a home by 3-6%.

Q2: How does the size of the extra garage influence its impact on a property’s value?#

A2: The size of the extra garage plays a significant role in determining its impact on the property’s value. Generally, larger garages tend to add more value than smaller ones. A two-car garage, for example, is typically more desirable and valuable than a one-car garage.

Q3: Does the type of extra garage affect its contribution to the property’s value?#

A3: Yes, the type of extra garage can also influence its impact on the property’s value. Attached garages, which are directly connected to the main house, are generally more valuable than detached garages. Additionally, garages with finished interiors, such as those that are insulated and have drywall, tend to add more value than unfinished garages.

Q4: How does the condition of the extra garage influence its contribution to the property’s value?#

A4: The condition of the extra garage is also a factor that affects its impact on the property’s value. A well-maintained garage that is in good condition will add more value than one that is in need of repairs or renovations.

Q5: In which specific scenarios can an extra garage add the most value to a property?#

A5: An extra garage can add the most value to a property in scenarios where there is a high demand for parking space, such as in urban areas or neighborhoods with limited street parking. Additionally, an extra garage can be particularly valuable for properties that are used for business purposes or for those that have multiple vehicles.
