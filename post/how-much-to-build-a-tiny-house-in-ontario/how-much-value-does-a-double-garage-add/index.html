How Much Value Does a Double Garage Add to Your Property?

This article aims to provide crucial insights into the impact of double garages on property value. How Much Value Does a Double Garage Add to Your Property?#

When it comes to boosting your property’s value, a double garage is a no-brainer. Think of it as an investment that pays off big time. Not only does it provide a safe and secure haven for your vehicles, but it also adds functionality and enhances the overall appeal of your home. Let’s dive into how a double garage can amplify your property value:

1. Curb Appeal:#

A double garage instantly elevates your home’s curb appeal, making it more attractive to potential buyers. It adds a touch of symmetry and balance to the facade, creating a sense of grandeur and spaciousness. Plus, a well-maintained garage door can complement the architectural style of your home, enhancing its visual appeal.

2. Storage and Functionality:#

With a double garage, you gain valuable storage space for vehicles, outdoor equipment, tools, and other belongings. This not only declutters your living space but also provides a dedicated area for hobbies and projects. The additional space can also be utilized as a workshop, gym, or even a home office, expanding your home’s functionality and livability.

3. Resale Value:#

A double garage is a highly sought-after feature by homebuyers, as it offers convenience, security, and extra storage. When it’s time to sell, your property will stand out from the competition, attracting more potential buyers who are willing to pay a premium for this valuable amenity. Studies have shown that homes with double garages command higher prices compared to those without.

4. Enhanced Security:#

A double garage provides an extra layer of security for your vehicles and belongings. Its sturdy construction and secure door system act as a deterrent against theft and vandalism. This not only protects your assets but also gives you peace of mind, knowing that your prized possessions are safe and sound.

5. Future-Proofing Your Home:#

In today’s fast-paced world, adaptability is key. A double garage can easily adapt to changing needs and trends. Whether you’re looking to accommodate a growing family, pursue new hobbies, or even start a home-based business, the extra space and functionality of a double garage can seamlessly accommodate your evolving lifestyle.

6. Return on Investment:#

The cost of adding a double garage to your property may seem substantial initially, but it’s an investment that pays off handsomely in the long run. The increased value of your home, coupled with the convenience and functionality it offers, makes it a worthwhile investment that boosts your property’s overall worth.

How Much Value Does a Double Garage Add?#

The exact value added by a double garage can vary depending on several factors, including the location, size, and condition of the garage, as well as the overall property market. However, studies and real estate experts generally agree that a double garage can increase your home’s value by anywhere from 5% to 15%.

  • Size: A larger double garage will typically add more value than a smaller one.

  • Condition: A double garage in good condition will add more value than one in need of repairs.

How to find contractors and get permits.#

Finding the right contractor and obtaining the necessary permits are crucial steps in constructing a double garage. Whether you’re planning an addition to your existing home or building a detached garage, these guidelines will help ensure a smooth and successful process:

1. Research and Recommendations:

  • Investigate local contractors specializing in garage construction.
  • Seek recommendations from friends, neighbors, or online review platforms.
  • Check their licenses, insurance, and references to ensure reliability.

2. Communicate Clearly:

  • Have a clear idea of the garage’s size, design, and desired features.
  • Communicate your expectations and budget to potential contractors.

3. Get Multiple Quotes:

  • Obtain quotes from at least three contractors to compare pricing and services.

4. Review Contracts Thoroughly:

  • Carefully examine contracts for details like project timeline, payment schedule, and warranties.

5. Secure Necessary Permits:

  • Research local building codes and zoning regulations related to garage construction.
