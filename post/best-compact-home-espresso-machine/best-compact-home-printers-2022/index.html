Best Compact Home Printers 2022#

Compact home printers are a great option for those who need a printer that doesn’t take up a lot of space. If you’re looking for the best compact home printers 2022, here are the key factors to consider:

  • Print quality: Look for a printer with high-resolution printing, measured in dots per inch (dpi). The higher the dpi, the sharper the print quality.
  • Speed: Consider the printer’s print speed, measured in pages per minute (ppm). The faster the print speed, the less time you’ll spend waiting for your prints.
  • Connectivity: Make sure the printer has the connectivity options you need, such as Wi-Fi, Ethernet, or USB.
  • Features: Some printers offer additional features, such as automatic duplex printing, scanning, and copying.

To help you make a decision, I’ve compiled a list of the best compact home printers 2022. These printers offer a combination of print quality, speed, connectivity, and features that make them ideal for home use:

  • HP DeskJet 2755e: This all-in-one printer offers high-quality printing, fast print speeds, and a variety of connectivity options.
  • Canon PIXMA TR4620: This compact printer offers high-resolution printing, fast print speeds, and a variety of connectivity options.
  • Epson EcoTank ET-2800: This all-in-one printer offers high-quality printing, fast print speeds, and a refillable ink tank system.
  • Brother MFC-J497DW: This all-in-one printer combines high-quality printing with fast print speeds, a variety of connectivity options, and a range of features.
  • Xerox VersaLink C405: This compact color laser printer offers high-quality printing, fast print speeds, and a variety of connectivity options.

Ultimately, the best compact home printer for you will depend on your specific needs. Consider the print quality, speed, connectivity, and features that are important to you before making a purchase.

FAQ#

Q1: What are the most important factors to consider when selecting a compact home printer?#

A1: When selecting a compact home printer, the most important factors to consider are print quality, speed, and cost per page. Additionally, you should also consider features such as duplex printing, wireless connectivity, and mobile printing capabilities.

Q2: Which compact home printers offer the best print quality?#

A2: The best compact home printers that offer excellent print quality include the HP Envy 6055e, the Canon Pixma TR8620, and the Epson Expression Photo HD XP-15000. These printers are known for producing sharp and vibrant prints, making them ideal for both personal and professional use.

Q3: Which compact home printers are the fastest?#

A3: The fastest compact home printers include the Brother MFC-J6945DW, the HP OfficeJet Pro 9025e, and the Epson WorkForce WF-2860. These printers can print up to 30 pages per minute in black and 24 pages per minute in color, making them ideal for busy households and small offices.

Q4: Which compact home printers offer the lowest cost per page?#

A4: The compact home printers that offer the lowest cost per page include the HP DeskJet 2755e, the Canon Pixma TS5320, and the Epson EcoTank ET-2760. These printers use high-yield ink cartridges or ink tanks, which can significantly reduce the cost of printing over time.

Q5: Which compact home printers offer the best features?#

A5: The compact home printers that offer the best features include the HP Envy 7855e, the Brother MFC-J995DW, and the Epson Expression Photo HD XP-15000. These printers offer a wide range of features, including duplex printing, wireless connectivity, mobile printing capabilities, and automatic document feeders (ADFs).
