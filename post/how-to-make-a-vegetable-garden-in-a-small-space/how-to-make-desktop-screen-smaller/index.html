How to Make Desktop Screen Smaller#

In this fast-paced digital world, we spend countless hours staring at our computer screens. Whether you’re working, gaming, or simply browsing the web, having a screen size that’s just right for your needs is essential. If you find your desktop screen overwhelming or straining your eyes, you can easily adjust it to a more comfortable size.

Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you make your desktop screen smaller:

  1. Right-Click on the Desktop: Begin by right-clicking anywhere on your desktop screen. This will bring up a context menu with various options.

  2. Select “Display Settings”: From the context menu, choose the “Display Settings” option. This will open the Windows Display Settings window.

  3. Adjust the Display Resolution: In the Display Settings window, locate the “Resolution” section. Here, you’ll see a list of available screen resolutions. Choose a resolution that is lower than your current resolution. A lower resolution means a smaller screen size.

  4. Click “Apply”: Once you’ve selected a new resolution, click the “Apply” button. Windows will start applying the new settings. You may be prompted to confirm the changes. Click “Yes” to proceed.

  5. Review the New Screen Size: After applying the new resolution, your desktop screen will shrink in size. Take a moment to review the new screen size and ensure it’s comfortable for your eyes. If not, you can always adjust it further by repeating steps 2-4.

Remember, the ideal screen size depends on your personal preferences and the tasks you perform on your computer. Experiment with different resolutions until you find one that optimizes your viewing experience and productivity.

Change resolution#

In this guide, you will learn how to adjust your desktop screen resolution to your preferences, ensuring optimal viewing and a better user experience.

Key Takeaways:#

  • Customize your desktop screen size to suit your specific needs.
  • Enhance your viewing experience by optimizing the screen resolution.
  • Easily adjust the screen resolution through simple steps.
  • Troubleshoot common issues related to screen resolution.

Steps to Adjust Desktop Screen Resolution:#

  1. Access Display Settings: Right-click anywhere on your desktop, select “Display Settings” from the context menu.

  2. Locate Resolution Options: In the Display Settings window, look for the “Resolution” or “Display resolution” section.

  3. Choose Your Desired Resolution: From the available options, select the screen resolution you prefer. Generally, higher numbers indicate higher resolution and sharper image quality.

  4. Apply Changes: Click “Apply” or “OK” to save the new screen resolution. You may be prompted to confirm the change.

  5. Check Results: Once applied, the screen will briefly go black and then reappear with the new resolution.

Troubleshooting:#

  1. Screen Blurry or Pixelated: If you experience blurry or pixelated visuals after changing the resolution, try a lower resolution.

  2. Items Appear Too Small or Large: If elements on your screen appear too small or large, check the scaling settings in the Display Settings window.

  3. No Available Resolution Options: If you don’t see suitable resolution options, update your graphics card drivers.

  4. Black Screen: If the screen goes black and doesn’t return after changing the resolution, wait a few minutes for it to automatically revert to the previous settings.

  5. Custom Resolution Not Supported: Some monitors may not support custom resolution settings. Check the manufacturer’s specifications.

Remember, the optimal screen resolution depends on the size and capabilities of your monitor. For the best viewing experience, consider your monitor’s recommended resolution.

Step 1: Access Display Settings

  1. Right-click on an empty area of your desktop and select “Display Settings” from the context menu.
  2. Alternatively, you can press the “Windows key + I” to open the Settings app and then click on “System” followed by “Display.”

Step 2: Adjust Display Scaling

  1. Under the “Display” section, locate the “Scale and layout” option.
  2. Click on the drop-down menu and select a lower scaling percentage, such as 90%, 85%, or 80%.
  3. The lower the percentage, the smaller your screen elements will appear.

Step 3: Apply Changes

  1. Click on the “Apply” button to save the changes.
  2. Your screen will briefly flicker as the resolution is adjusted.

  1. If you find that the text is still too small or too large, you can adjust the text size independently.
  2. Under the “Display” section, click on the “Advanced scaling settings” link.
  3. Adjust the “Custom scaling” percentage to your liking.

Step 5: Check for Optimal Viewing Distance

  1. Ensure that your eyes are positioned approximately an arm’s length away from the screen.
  2. This optimal viewing distance helps reduce eye strain and promotes healthier computing habits.

Step 6: Update Graphics Drivers

  1. Regularly update your graphics drivers to ensure compatibility with your display and operating system.
  2. Outdated drivers can cause various display issues, including incorrect scaling and resolution problems.

Use Third-Party Software#

There’s an easier way to shrink your screen, and that’s through third-party software. Several reputable programs can help you adjust your screen size without hassles. Let’s explore some popular options:

DisplayFusion#

DisplayFusion is a feature-rich program that offers extensive control over your display settings. Not only can you adjust the resolution and scaling, but you can also create custom display profiles, manage multiple monitors, and even control your desktop wallpaper. Wondering how to make your desktop screen smaller with DisplayFusion? Just follow these steps:

  1. Download and install DisplayFusion.

  2. Launch DisplayFusion and click on the “Settings” tab.

  3. In the “Display Settings” section, adjust the “Resolution” and “Scaling” options to your desired values.

  4. Click on the “Apply” button to save your changes.

EasyRes#

EasyRes is a lightweight and user-friendly program that lets you quickly change your screen resolution. It’s a great option if you don’t need all the bells and whistles of DisplayFusion. Here’s how to achieve a smaller screen with EasyRes:

  1. Download and install EasyRes.

  2. Launch EasyRes and select your desired resolution from the list.

  3. Click on the “Apply” button to apply the new resolution.

Screen Resolution Changer#

Screen Resolution Changer is a simple and straightforward program that does exactly what its name suggests - it modifies your screen resolution. It’s a no-fuss option for those who want a basic tool to adjust their display settings. And the steps involved are fairly straightforward:

  1. Begin by acquiring and installing Screen Resolution Changer.

  2. Launch the program, and you’ll be presented with a list of available resolutions.

  3. Select the one you want and hit “Apply” to implement the change.

Custom Resolution Utility (CRU)#

Custom Resolution Utility (CRU) is a more advanced program that gives you complete control over your display settings. It’s not as user-friendly as the other options mentioned here, but it allows you to create custom resolutions that aren’t normally available. If that’s what you’re looking for, here’s what to do:

  1. Download and install CRU.

  2. Launch CRU and select your monitor from the list.

  3. Click on the “Detailed Resolutions” button.

  4. In the “Detailed Resolutions” window, you can create a custom resolution by entering the desired values for width, height, and refresh rate.

  5. Click on the “Add” button to save your custom resolution.

  6. Click on the “Apply” button to apply the new resolution.

FAQ#

Q1: What are the different ways to make my desktop screen smaller?#

A1: There are several methods to reduce the size of your desktop screen. You can adjust the display settings, change the screen resolution, use keyboard shortcuts, or employ third-party software. The specific method that works best for you depends on your operating system and personal preferences.

Q2: How can I adjust my display settings to make my screen smaller?#

A2: To adjust your display settings and reduce the screen size, follow these steps:

  1. Right-click on an empty area of your desktop.
  2. Select “Display Settings” or “Screen Resolution” from the menu.
  3. Locate the “Resolution” or “Scale” option.
  4. Select a lower resolution or scale percentage to make the screen elements smaller.
  5. Click “Apply” or “OK” to save the changes.

Q3: What is the keyboard shortcut for making my screen smaller?#

A3: The keyboard shortcut for reducing the screen size varies depending on your operating system:

  • Windows: Hold down the “Ctrl” key and press the “-” (minus) key.
  • Mac: Hold down the “Command” key and press the “-” (minus) key.

Q4: Can I use third-party software to make my screen smaller?#

A4: Yes, you can use third-party software to adjust the screen size and zoom level. Some popular options include:

  • ZoomIt: Allows you to zoom in and out of specific areas of your screen.
  • Magnifier: A built-in Windows tool that lets you magnify the entire screen or a portion of it.
  • Screen Magnifier: A Mac app that provides various magnification options.

Q5: What should I do if the screen is still too large after making adjustments?#

A5: If the screen remains too large after trying the above methods, you may need to check your monitor’s settings or consult your device manufacturer for additional solutions. You can also try updating your graphics card drivers or experimenting with different display scaling options.
