A2: To locate reputable developers known for offering affordable house and land packages in Brisbane, you can research online review platforms, consult with local real estate agents, and attend property expos or seminars. A4: When purchasing a house and land package in Brisbane, you must consider various costs beyond the purchase price, such as stamp duty, legal fees, conveyancing fees, and pest and building inspections.
